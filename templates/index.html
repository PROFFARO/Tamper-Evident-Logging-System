These operations directly manipulate database records to simulate real-world attacks. After tampering, use Verify Chain to observe detection.

Modification Attack

Directly modify a log entry's description in the database, bypassing the hash chain. The original hash becomes invalid.

Deletion Attack

Remove a log entry from the database entirely. This creates a gap in the sequential IDs and breaks the hash chain linkage.

Reorder Attack

Swap the content of two log entries to simulate reordering. The hash chain and timestamp ordering will be violated.
